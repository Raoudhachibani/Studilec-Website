OUR CLUBS

Inelectronic Student Club

The INELECTRONICS students' club is a dynamic organization dedicated to connecting university students with the thriving electronics industry in Algeria. Our primary goal is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world applications by fostering partnerships with leading businesses in the field. Through our initiatives, we provide students with invaluable opportunities to engage with industry professionals, gain practical experience, and access career-enhancing resources. By joining our club, members can network with like-minded peers, participate in innovative projects, and unlock doors to exciting prospects in the electronics sector. Whether you're an aspiring engineer, a technology enthusiast, or simply curious about the intersection of academia and industry, the INELECTRONICS students' club offers a vibrant community where learning meets opportunity. Join us as we embark on a journey to shape the future of electronics together!

Wameedh Scientific Club

Wameedh, a scientific club affiliated with IGEE (formerly INELEC), is dedicated to fostering technical prowess and innovation among its members. Through collaborative projects and hands-on workshops, we provide students with opportunities to enhance their skills while addressing real-world challenges. Our focus extends beyond academia as we strive to make a positive impact on our community through outreach programs and innovative solutions. Whether you're an experienced enthusiast or someone intrigued by science and technology, Wameedh welcomes you to join our vibrant community where exploration, innovation, and social impact converge.

IEEE Club

The IEEE Student Branch University of Boumerdes is a beacon of inspiration, inviting individuals from all backgrounds to join our global community dedicated to advancing technology for the betterment of humanity. Through a diverse array of activities including conferences, workshops, competitions, and educational initiatives, we provide opportunities for continuous learning and skill development. Whether you're a student exploring the world of electronics, an aspiring engineer, or simply curious about technology, our club offers a supportive environment where you can collaborate with like-minded peers and access invaluable resources and mentorship. Join us as we embark on a journey of innovation, discovery, and growth, shaping a brighter future for generations to come.
